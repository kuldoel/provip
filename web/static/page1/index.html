The Pathway platform is now in its pilot phase. At this stage we are focusing its use on study programmes and companies in the field of IT and marketing. Should you be interested to test it in another context, please contact the project coordinator.

Study Programme

Interested study programmes can send a message to the platform administrator, applying for access. They will be notified of acceptance by mail.

Company

Companies looking for international interns can register directly on the platform and start creating and offering internships positions right away.

Student

Students can only register if they participate in one of the registered study programmes. The student's registration needs to be approved by the study programme administrator before he can log-in.
