Pathway has been developed as a tool to support the internship process. Even though document sharing is possible, the platform is not intended as a system for content collection. The privacy of the internship remains subject of the agreement between the student and the company. The charter produced by the platform is not intended as a legally binding contract but as a guide through the internship. Official institutional or national procedures regarding internships still need to be taken into account and are subject of agreement between the different stakeholders.

Pathway uses standard web security protocols and guidelines in the development of the pilot phase platform. Pathway or the project consortium can not be held liable for any damage encountered in the use of the platform.
